KARACHI EAT FESTIVAL

The Karachi Eat Festival is one of the city's most anticipated events, blending food and music in a vibrant celebration. Held annually, the festival features a diverse range of culinary delights from over 120 food vendors, many of whom are home-based entrepreneurs.

SOUL-FEST KARACHI

The SoulFest Festival is one of Karachi's most anticipated events, blending vibrant music performances with the city's diverse culinary offerings. Held at Beach View Park, the festival invites visitors to enjoy an exciting three-day celebration featuring renowned Pakistani artists.
